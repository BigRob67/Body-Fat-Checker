AI Body Fat Estimator

The Problem

Many people and insurance companies need a quick way to estimate body fat levels, because body fat percentage is correlated with long-term health outcomes. Traditional methods require expensive equipment or skilled technicians.

Who Benefits?

  • Insurance companies evaluating general wellness risk levels
  • Fitness and nutrition app users tracking progress over time
  • Individuals who want a quick visual estimate of body fat category

How the Model Works

This website uses a custom Teachable Machine image model trained on visual examples of different body fat ranges. When a user stands in front of the webcam, the AI predicts which category they most resemble based on patterns learned during training.

The 4 model classes:

  • 0–10%
  • 10–20%
  • 20–30%
  • Fat

Instructions

1. Stand about 3–6 feet away from your camera.
2. Make sure your shirt is on (school appropriate).
3. Press “Start Camera”.
4. The model will update predictions live.
